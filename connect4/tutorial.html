How to play Connect 4

Controls:

Use arrow keys to select a column.

Press space to drop a token on the column.

Rules:

Place a token in a column.

Tokens stack on each other, instead of being placed in a certain spot.

A board is 6 x 7 spots.

The Objective:

As the title says, "Connect 4". Place 4 tokens vertically, horizontally, or diagonally to win.
